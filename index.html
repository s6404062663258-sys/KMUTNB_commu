KMUTNB Student Connect

Online Community and Activity Matching Platform for Students

Verify securely with a University Google account. Discover information, create activities, find peers, and communicate safely. Connect, exchange ideas, and collaborate on projects, study sessions, sports, and university events.

Problem Statement

Students lack an effective way to discover and join university activities that match their interests and skills.

Lack of Trust

Public social media groups do not verify users through university email accounts. The identity of posters and the reliability of shared information cannot always be confirmed.

Unorganized Content

Different types of posts are mixed in a single feed without clear categories, making relevant information difficult to find.

Difficulty Finding Partners

Posts seeking activity participants are mixed with general community posts, making activities difficult to discover and manage.

No Complete Workflow

Existing social media groups lack a structured process for activity recommendations, join requests, approvals, membership management, and activity-specific communication.

Project Objectives

Build a categorized community for verified students.

Support the creation and management of university-related activities.

Recommend activities using explainable Hybrid Matching.

Connect requests, approvals, membership, and activity chat.

Support accountable anonymous posting.

Provide reporting, admin review, and moderation records.

The system has two main user roles: Verified Student and Administrator. A Verified Student can act as both an activity participant and an activity organizer.

01 PRIMARY USER

Verified Students

Community User

Creates profiles, browses categorized posts, creates normal or anonymous posts, comments, reports inappropriate content, and tracks report status.

Activity Participant

Discovers activities, receives rule-based recommendations, views match reasons, requests to join, tracks request status, accesses activity chat after approval, and submits feedback.

Activity Owner

Creates and manages activities, defines required interests and skills, reviews join requests, manages members, communicates through activity chat, completes activities, and reviews feedback.

02 ADMIN

Administrator

An authorized user responsible for maintaining platform safety and integrity.

  • Reviewing user reports
  • Moderating content
  • Managing report status
  • Investigating anonymous posts (when permitted)
  • Handling account restrictions
  • Maintaining immutable audit logs

Scoring Algorithm Example

User Profile Bio:

"Interested in building websites and designing user experiences."

Activity Requirement:

"Looking for peers to help with UI/UX for a Web Application."

Rule Score = Skill 40% + Interest 30% + Major 15% + Year 15%
Semantic Score = Cosine similarity (Profile Vector, Activity Vector) × 100
Hybrid Score = (Rule Score × 0.80) + (Semantic Score × 0.20)

The Golden Rule: Accountability

Every critical administrative decision (e.g., banning a user, deleting a post, or revealing an anonymous author) requires a mandatory reason and is permanently recorded in the append-only Audit Log.
